## Ingredients

- 1 cup olive oil
- 1/2 cup good-tasting vinegar
- 1/2 tsp salt
- various mix-ins
  - black pepper
  - red pepper flakes
  - Italian seasonsing
  - mustard
  - honey

## Directions

1. Add all ingredients to a mason jar with a lid.
2. Shake to combine.

## Source

This is my (Jack's) preferred ratio of oli to vinegar. I (and Mom, I believe) like a more acidic vinaigrette, so keep that in mind.
