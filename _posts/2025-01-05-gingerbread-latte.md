## Ingredients

- 1-2 shots espresso
- 1/2 cup milk, steamed
- 1 Tablespoon (1/2 oz) [gingerbread syrup](/gingerbread-syrup.html)

## Directions

Add syrup to cup. Pull shot(s) into cup. Add steamed milk to cup.
