## Ingredients

- 2 ounces mezcal
- 1 ounce fresh lime juice
- 1 ounce simple syrup (or agave syrup)

## Directions

Combine with ice. Shake. Strain.

## Source

I took the "Tommy's Margarita" recipe from *Simonson, Robert (2016) A Proper Drink* but edited it lightly for mezcal.
