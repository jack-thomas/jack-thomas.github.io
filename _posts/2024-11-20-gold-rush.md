## Ingredients

- 2 ounces whiskey, preferably Angel's Envy
- 1 ounce lemon juice
- 1 ounce [honey syrup](/honey-syrup.html)

## Directions

Combine with ice. Shake. Strain. (Or just build in the glass and stir.)
