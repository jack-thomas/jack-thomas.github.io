## Ingredients

- 4 cups water
- 6-8 apples
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 2 cups white sugar
- 4 cinnamon sticks
- splash vanilla

## Directions

Simmer until apples are soft. Strain.

## Sources

Jack Thomas and Casey Wharton. We came up with this syrup when we were trying to figure out a way to use up some extra apples.
