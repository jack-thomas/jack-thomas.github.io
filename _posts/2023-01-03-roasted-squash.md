## Ingredients

- 2+ medium acorn squash
- Olive oil or butter
- Salt (optional)
- Brown sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 400F.
2. Prepare squash.
3. Bake about 30 to 45 minutes, depending on the size of the squash.
