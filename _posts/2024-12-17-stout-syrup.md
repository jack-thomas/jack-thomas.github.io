## Ingredients

- 2 cans Guinness
- 2 cups white sugar
- 1 cup demerara sugar

## Directions

Heat until sugar is dissolved and bubbles become big and slow. (NOte that there is a point where it will foam up rather suddenly, so pay attention.)
