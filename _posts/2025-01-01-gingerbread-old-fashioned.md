## Ingredients

- 2 ounces whiskey, preferably Angel's Envy
- 3/4 ounce [Gingerbread Syrup](/gingerbread-syrup.html)
- 2-4 dashes Fee Brothers Black Walnut Bitters
- Garnish: cherry (optional)

## Directions

Build over ice. Garnish (if you feel like it).
